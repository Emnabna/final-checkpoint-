How can reading become a passion ?


To be honest , i don't know , i used to look at people who read like what do you meannnnn , How can words have such an impact on a person's behavior . Well ,Something in life just happens and brings up a fair amount of change which takes you from a point to another and from there to move on on on on! And the same happened when I became passionate about reading books(irrespective of syllabus ones). That gave me a different taste,even now ,offering me a delicious flavour . But ,let's go into the past, the more I tasted the more I wanted. There was no way to control my irresistible love for reading. Okay ,drop it here and come to the point of my story or journey whatever you say ---it's optional. The time was when i knew nothing about books or something related to it. So,my journey goes like ONE BOOK , that one book that changes your perspective of reading , of life, of existing so after reading that ONE BOOK I had come to know about books other than syllabus stuffs . some books were ,to wit: the adventure of tom sawyer ,I'm Malala, namsake and many more. Still I kept on reading,even if I understood 2words or 2 sentences or nothing else, I didn't quit .

I had read somewhere that Reading is imperceptible when you read ,you find no change. "it's just simple and ordinary but with passage of time ,it carves you and shake the unshaken potential dwelling inside you; which just kept me motivated.
